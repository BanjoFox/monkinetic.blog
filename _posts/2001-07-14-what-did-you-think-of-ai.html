I was mostly disappointed. I thought there were a lot of interesting questions and ideas that could have been explored, but as usual, Speilberg/Hollywood took the easy way out. There were also a lot of little gaps left in the plot that were just annoying. "A.I" had the possibility of being great, but was only pablum.
