--- title: "Do not meddle in the affairs of dragons" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a55cdafa970b" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2001/11/do-not-meddle-in-the-affairs-of-dragons.html" --- David spots a great bumper sticker:
"Avoid meddling in affairs of dragons
for you are crunchy and taste good with ketchup."
Some of my readers may not know this is a clever send-up of a famous quote from J. R. R. Tolkien's classic "Fellowship Of The Ring":
"Do not meddle in the affairs of wizards, for they are subtle and quick to anger."
