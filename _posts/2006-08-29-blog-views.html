--- title: "Blog views" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a5b3628c970c" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2006/08/blog-views.html" --- While reading [blogHelper](http://bloghelper.is-there.net/)'s post about using [Wordpress](http://wordpress.org) as a CMS, I noticed the menu of views in the upper right corner. You can select "normal" (blog style), "discussions" (most commented posts), "book" (all posts arranged into a hierarchical book structure), or "best" (highest rated posts). I thought this was a very clever and interesting way to expose users to various aspects of the content.
