--- title: "Presidential Speech and Document Tag Clouds" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a5b366bf970c" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2006/11/presidential-speech-and-document-tag-clouds.html" --- I'm a little over the whole "[tag cloud](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tag_cloud)" (a simple weighted list, really) thing as applied to blog posts, but applying it to more socially "interesting" texts is a clever idea. Here [Chirag Mehta](http://chir.ag/) has run a series of tag clouds on 360 speeches and documents by Presidents of the United States - [US Presidential Speeches Tag Cloud](http://chir.ag/phernalia/preztags/). For example, move the slider back and forth to move forward and back in time, and compare W's *2001-01-20: First Inaugural Address* with his *2006-01-31: State of the Union Address*. Pretty cool.
