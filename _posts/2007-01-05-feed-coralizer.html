--- title: "Feed Coralizer" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a5b36a92970c" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2007/01/feed-coralizer.html" --- If you find yourself behind a firewall or filter that blocks popular podcast file servers like libsyn, try out the [Feed Coralizer](http://deliciouslymeta.com/projects/coralizer/coralizer.php). The Feed Coralizer rewrites the feed, routing (mp3/m4a/m4v/wav/aiff/wma/wmv/mov/mp4/mpeg/avi/pdf) files through the [Coral network](http://coralcdn.org). I have discovered that certain networks do not allow Coral proxying: revision3.com (Diggnation) and dl.tv are two that I know of right now. Comments and suggestions can be left on this post.
