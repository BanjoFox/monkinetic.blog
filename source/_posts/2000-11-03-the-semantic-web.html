Semantic has to do with meaning - in this case, how various technologies are being used to encode the meaning of the web, thus making the web even more machine readable. Why bother? When computers can parse out more of the meaning of the content on the web, they can help us extract more information about what's out there - making it easier to get it here - whever here may be.
