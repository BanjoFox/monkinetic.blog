--- title: "Fires in Arizona" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a5b35d85970c" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2002/06/fires-in-arizona.html" --- The "Rodeo" and "Chedeski" fires in north eastern Arizona have now eaten through 300,000 acres of high country forest.
The fire has burned about 300,000 acres - about 479 square miles - since Tuesday. As many as 25,000 people have fled homes from more than half a dozen towns.
