--- title: "Ah, the terror of Technorati" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a55ce0fe970b" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2003/01/ah-the-terror-of-technorati.html" --- I should never pay attention to the weblog popularity contests/ link-tracking sites like Technorati, because I'm not a popular blogger and will likely never be. I'm interested in too many things, and get distracted too easily. I'm rarely effectively witty, and too clever for my own good. I don't write too badly, but I'll never win an award for it, and the only time I'm gaspingly passionate is when I'm about to stick my foot in my mouth. Therefore, seeing that Technorati says only 6 other bloggers are linking to me should be absolutely no surprise. (Even checking my former blog self only reveals 18 links). Stupid popular bloggers. -)
