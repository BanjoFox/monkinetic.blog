--- title: "TiVo Trick: Stream internet radio to your entertainment system!" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a5b36554970c" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2004/10/tivo-trick-stream-internet-radio-to-your-entertainment-system.html" --- Working from home today, and listening to some nice european trance in the background... through my entertainment system. How? 1. Find a nice internet radio stream in [iTunes](http://www.apple.com/itunes) 2. Add the stream to a playlist. This is generally not useful, as the stream never "ends", so your playlist will never go to the "next" song. Be patient. 3. Install the [TiVo Desktop software](http://www.tivo.com/4.9.4.1.asp) on your Mac. 4. Use the TiVo Desktop preference pane to tell your Mac to share your internet radio playlist. 5. In the TiVo, go to Music and Photos > (your computer) > (your playlist) > (your stream) 6. Play!
