--- title: "The Things GTD app looks interesting" layout: post tp_urlid: "6a010534988cd3970b0120a5b36b12970c" tp_favoritecount: 0 tp_commentcount: 0 tp_permalink: "http://www.monkinetic.com/2007/11/the-things-gtd-app-looks-interesting.html" --- Check out [this screencast](http://tagamac.com/2007/11/things_screencast/) from [tagamac.com](http://tagamac.com) on [Things](http://culturedcode.com/things/), a new GTD app from [Cultured Code](http://culturedcode.com/). I particularly like the concept of "Areas" - "an ongoing region of activity" - to group both Projects (multistep goals) and Tasks (single-step goals) together into something you can focus on. The example in the screencast is "Home Improvement", an Area that contains both a Project ("fix the storm door") and several single-step tasks.
